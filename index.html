Welcome to ACS at Monas
This is the semi-official website for the ACS club at Monas University.

If you did not know already, ACS stands for
Arts Club and Society
at Monas of course.

Our Goals

Networking

We aim to make networking easy in our society. Meet new people, and possibly make new friends!

Knowledge

We aim to provide knowledge on various topics related not only to Arts but other fields as well.

Good Times

We aim to host some recreational events, including sports, food, and/or more!
